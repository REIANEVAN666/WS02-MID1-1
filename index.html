KORO-SENSEI

ABOUT KORO

Acts as the homeroom teacher of Class 3-E of Kunugigaoka Junior High School, and the anti-heroic supporting protagonist of Assassination Classroom. He claimed to be responsible for creating the permanent crescent moon, and added that he planned to destroy the earth after teaching Class 3-E for a year.
